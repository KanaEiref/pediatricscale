Topic: Health

Goal: A single feature digital product.

Product: Pediatric Scale

Problem: The current two types of pediatric scales in pediatrician's office are very hard to use.

Parents have hard ways to measure a wiggly baby at home, they have to wait the 3-month or 6-month checkup points to know baby's weight, because there is no better way to know the accurate number of baby's weight at home.

Parents need to know the accurate number of the baby in order to know what they did wrong in terms of feeding..etc.

New ideas: try to find a better shape to contain the squirmy baby

fish tank shape, lotus flower shape , open jaw shape

Problem still not solved: not comfortable for babies, whoever measures the baby need to try to comfort the baby while read the numbers on the LCD monitor. New Idea: a sling, soft fabric

Two ideas are generated here:

A LCD monitor attached to the sling, OR

An smart mobile phone case that hookup both monitor and the baby.

Either way, the weight censor will be on the sling

At a check-up meeting, Steve Hickey suggested blue tooth idea which separates teh measuring device with the monitor device.

After calculating the cost, if we use the same resolution level LCD monitor, it is more cost effective to use bluetooth-smart phone approach than using the LCD_on_sling approach.

Decision --> Bluetooth approach -- > Next: Prototype

Using HTML5/CSS3/Javascript to develop different interfaces.

Using faces to indicate if the baby is gaining weight or losing weight.
